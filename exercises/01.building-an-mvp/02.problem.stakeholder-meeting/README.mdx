# Stakeholder Meeting

👨‍💼 You now have a prepared discovery brief. Next, run the stakeholder meeting to
turn open questions into concrete decisions for a realistic use case: friends
from different organizations trying to coordinate lunch or a D&D session.

🐨 Open <InlineFile file="discovery-brief.md" /> and update it live during the
conversation.

Meeting goals:

1. Resolve key unknowns with stakeholder input
2. Confirm in-scope and out-of-scope boundaries
3. Clarify success criteria and failure signals
4. Capture the highest risks and initial mitigations

In this live workshop, your facilitator plays the stakeholders and answers your
questions.

💰 Keep the conversation iterative:

- Ask one high-leverage question
- Capture the answer
- Ask the best follow-up
- Record the decision that changed
