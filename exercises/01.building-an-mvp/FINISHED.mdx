# Building an MVP

Great work finishing Exercise 1.

Before moving on, complete this reflection as a required output.

## Required reflection outputs

1. **Question hindsight**
   - What question do you wish you had asked earlier?
   - Why would that question have changed scope, risk, or implementation choices?

2. **Scope brief**
   - In-scope MVP outcomes (what you intentionally optimized for)
   - Out-of-scope items you deliberately deferred
   - "Build vs buy vs defer" decision and one-sentence justification

3. **Constraint reflection**
   - Top constraints that shaped your decisions (time, team, dependency, data/privacy, technical)
   - Which constraint was most underestimated at first?
   - What tradeoff did you make because of those constraints?

4. **Outcome alignment check**
   - Which success criteria were clear before building?
   - Which assumptions were validated, invalidated, or still unknown?
   - If you repeated this exercise, what would you change first?
